Handmade Templates

Info

Kirsten Spruit is a designer who codes websites by hand, whether they are small artistic pieces in the context of the poetic web, or larger commissioned platforms like FABER's Bright Cityscapes website.

For this year’s Faberland Kirsten has developed a special time-based HTML/CSS/Javascript template package, with which you can create your own handmade one-page website or link-in-bio tool that can change look depending on the time of day.

The package includes instructions to help you customise the templates and add content directly in the code, and host your website online via Github. Prior experience with coding is a plus, but not required — although a willingness to learn some basics is essential.

The digital package comes with a limited edition RISO print.
